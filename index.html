Presentation of Gorilla

Gorilla, the Gorillas, is a genus of monkeys belonging to the family Hominidae. These are the closest monkeys to humans after chimpanzees, since gorilla DNA is 98.4% identical to that of humans. Gorilla is the largest of the eight genera of the hominoid super-family. Males, in particular, can develop a colossal physical strength. Gorillas live in groups in tropical or subtropical forests, where they feed on plants and sometimes on insects. Their presence covers a small percentage of Africa and they are endangered species. On the found at very varied altitudes. The mountain gorilla lives in the cloud forests of the Virunga Mountains, from an altitude of 2,200 to 4,300 meters. Lowland gorillas live in dense forests and lowland swamps.
